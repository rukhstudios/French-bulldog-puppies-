  • A Locus: AY/at (fawn/sable, carries tan points)
  • D Locus: d/d (2 copies for dilute base coat)
  • E Locus: carries cream/yellow + mask (negative for ancient red)
  • S Locus: n/S (pied/white spotting carrier)
  • Hair length: L/l4 (carries 1 copy of l4 long hair allele)
Quick genetics notes (plain English)
  • A Locus (Agouti): controls fawn/sable, tan points, and solids (e.g., AY for fawn)
  • D Locus (Dilute): lightens black to blue (or chocolate to lilac)
  • E Locus (Extension): controls red/yellow pigment; “ee” (cream) can mask other colors
  • S Locus (Piebald): creates white spotting (pied/parti)
